Ethical Issues

The Ethical use of Personal Information by Governments, Businesses, and Hackers

  1. Identity Theft - By being online and on social media you are in danger of having your identity stolen. Someone can take all your information and they can become you. Also, your identity can be stolen with all your information like your address and sell it on the black market like some item.
  2. Phishing - It is when a website seems to be real but in reality it is a fake that is trying to get your information. This is ethically wrong to act and pretend to be a real website and app but then be fake and steal personal data from a person.
  3. Sharing Email - When you send someone an email. Is it right to share that email with other people, there are no protocol to protecting your email if you sent it to someone.
  4. Webcam - Just because you had signed an agreement of use it does not make it ethically right to turn on the webcam of your phone, computer, or laptop and watch people through that camera.
  5. Ad Pop-Ups - It is not okay for companies to track your movements whenever you are on the web. Anything you search up or any website you visit is collected. Then you get personalized ads made to your interests. It is wrong for companies to track your movements online and then take that information to make ads for you.
